Interests


My interests began in GIScience when I saw the broad applications and unique communication abilities of geovisualization. I am passionate about open science and favor open source technology.
I believe open science goes in tandem with community science and making science as a discpline and science communciation accessible.

My interests in climate change are related to my personal philosphy of love and respect of nature, animals and other people. Climate change is already impacts peoples across the globe, the impacts disproportiantely impact marginalized communities. Using geovisualization to understand climate change impacts and adaptation impacts I hope to highlight geographic areas that need investment to face these impacts. Additionally, I seek to make this knowledge easily accessible through an web application focused on user experience.
I believe political ecology, critical physcial geography, and more than human geographies can be employed as frameworks for understanding climate change while framing environmental justice.
