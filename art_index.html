Art: Acrylics, Fiber Arts, Watercolor & Collage

Art

My art inspiration comes from artists such as: Brian and Wendy Froud, Arthur Rackham, Hayao Miyazaki, my grandparents Rex and Martha Slack and the many contemporary artists whose work I follow. I am also inspired by the New Mexico landscape, and the public art in Albuquerque. The creation of art is a calming yet exciting experience for me, my busy mind can turn off and channel itself onto the page.
